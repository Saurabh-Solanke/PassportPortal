Passport Application FAQs

Here are answers to frequently asked questions about passport applications.

Here’s a detailed overview of the passport making charges in India for both new applications and re-issuance:

Passport fees in India depend on the type of passport and the applicant’s age. As of current rates, the fees are as follows:

  • For a 36-page passport booklet, the fee is Rs. 1500 for normal applications and Rs. 3500 for Tatkaal applications.
  • For a 60-page passport booklet, the fee is Rs. 2000 for normal applications and Rs. 4000 for Tatkaal applications.

When applying for a passport in India, you typically need the following documents:

  • Proof of identity (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, etc.)
  • Proof of address (Electricity bill, Rent agreement, etc.)
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • Additional documents as required depending on specific circumstances (marriage certificate, etc.)

You can track your passport application status on our portal in history section, You can also track status through the official Passport Seva website. You'll need your application number and date of birth to check the status.

The time taken to process a passport application varies based on the type of application and other factors. Typically, it can take from 10 to 45 days for a normal passport application and 1 to 3 days for a Tatkaal application.

Yes, you can submit a passport application online through our portal or the Passport Seva Portal. This option allows you to fill out the form, upload documents, and schedule appointments.
